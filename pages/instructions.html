Getting Started

Welcome! Follow these steps to set up your Capuzzella site and start building.

1

Change Your Password

Your account was created with an initial password. For security, please change it right away in Settings → Account. Choose a strong, unique password that you don't use on other sites.

When you're done, come back to this page and continue with step 2.

2

Bookmark /console as Your Dashboard

The /console page is your Dashboard — the home screen you land on after signing in. From here you can jump to Pages, Assets, the Design System, and Skills, check your AI provider at a glance, and see which pages are published, changed, or still drafts. Bookmark it in your browser for quick access whenever you need to work on your site.

3

Write Your First Prompt in the Editor Panel

You see the Editor Panel on the right side of the screen (if not enable it). This is called the Edit Mode. Add ?edit=true to any page URL to enable it.

For your first prompt, describe the site you want in plain language. For example:

Create a new index.html page for my business.

Include:
- A hero section with a headline, short tagline, and a call-to-action button
- A brief about section
- Three service cards in a row
- A contact section with an email link

Keep it clean, professional, and mobile-friendly.

Click Build page and wait for the AI to finish. When it's done, click the Open new page button in the Editor Panel to view your new homepage in edit mode.
